Average Cost of Solar Panel Installation for Home

Wonder how much it would cost to install solar panels? No two solar energy systems are built the same. A solar power system is custom designed depending on the type of structure, roof specifications, shading, and utility. Solar panel home installation costs a national average of Rs. 1,89,000 to Rs. 2,15,000 for a 3kW solar panel system. The per-watt price for solar panel systems can range from Rs. 75 to Rs. 85 in India.

Below is a tabular representation of an estimated range of solar panel installation costs per kWh in India across different system sizes:
